BRAND: GreenPulse - Sustainable Energy Solutions

MISSION
GreenPulse exists to make clean energy accessible, affordable, and effortless for every Australian household. We believe the transition to renewable energy should feel like an upgrade, not a sacrifice.

VISION
By 2030, GreenPulse aims to power 500,000 homes across Australia with solar and battery solutions, reducing household carbon emissions by 2.1 million tonnes annually.

CORE VALUES
1. Simplicity First: We make complex energy technology feel effortless. No jargon, no confusion.
2. Radical Transparency: Every cost, every saving, every impact metric is visible to our customers.
3. Community Impact: We measure success not just in revenue, but in neighborhoods transformed.
4. Innovation with Purpose: We only adopt technology that meaningfully improves people's lives.
5. Local Pride: We are proudly Australian-made, Australian-installed, Australian-supported.

TARGET AUDIENCE
Primary: Homeowners aged 30-55 in suburban Australia, household income $90K-$180K. They care about reducing bills and doing the right thing for the environment but feel overwhelmed by solar options. They research thoroughly before buying and trust peer recommendations over advertising.

Secondary: Small business owners with commercial properties looking to reduce operational costs and demonstrate environmental responsibility to their customers.

Geographic Focus: Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane metro areas, expanding to Perth and Adelaide in 2026.
